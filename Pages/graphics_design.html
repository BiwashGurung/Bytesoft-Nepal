Position: Graphics Designer
Employment Type: Full-Time (Onsite)
Experience Level: Senior Graphics Designer
Position Available: 1 Position
Posting Date: June 20, 2025
Apply Before: June 27, 2025

Job Details

Lead and execute creative projects, overseeing design strategy and team collaboration. Play a crucial role in crafting innovative, visually impactful designs that meet client needs and elevate their brand presence.

Requirements

  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) and Figma.
  • Experience in branding, visual identity, and maintaining design consistency.
  • Strong creative thinking and ability to generate original design concepts.
  • Familiarity with UI/UX design principles for web and mobile platforms.
  • Keen attention to detail with a focus on flawless design execution.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills with cross-functional teams.

Responsibilities

  • Design engaging visuals for digital and print platforms, including websites and social media.
  • Collaborate with teams to develop and execute creative concepts that align with brand guidelines.
  • Create wireframes and mockups for web and mobile applications, focusing on UI/UX.
  • Revise and refine designs based on feedback to meet client and project goals.
  • Stay current with design trends to ensure innovative and competitive design solutions.
