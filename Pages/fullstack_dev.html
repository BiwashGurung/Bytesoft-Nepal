Position: Fullstack Developer
Employment Type: Full-Time (Onsite)
Experience Level: Senior Fullstack Developer
Position Available: 1 Position
Posting Date: June 20, 2025
Apply Before: June 27, 2025

Job Details

Lead and drive end-to-end development projects, balancing front-end and back-end requirements to deliver cohesive, high-performance applications. Play a vital role in building scalable, efficient, and secure solutions that align with client goals and enhance user experience across platforms.

Requirements

  • Proficiency in front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue).
  • Experience with server-side technologies (Node.js, Express, Django, or similar frameworks).
  • Knowledge of RESTful API design and integration, as well as third-party API consumption.
  • Strong understanding of version control (Git) and collaboration in code repositories (GitHub, GitLab).
  • Ability to implement and maintain responsive, user-centric web and mobile app design.
  • Strong problem-solving skills, with a focus on scalable and efficient code.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills to work effectively within cross-functional teams.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain responsive web applications with a focus on user experience and performance.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and deliver new features and enhancements.
  • Implement front-end interfaces and back-end services to create a seamless experience for users.
  • Debug and troubleshoot issues across the full stack, ensuring optimal application performance.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and technologies to recommend improvements and innovations.
